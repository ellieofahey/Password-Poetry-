Why do unique passwords even matter?

Having a strong, unique password is the first line of defense against malicious cyber threats. While it's somewhat common knowledge that a passowrd should be a random string of numbers, letters, and symbols, these passwords are actually easier for computer to guess than a string of a few random words or phrases.
